Senior Product Manager

Flutterwave
📍 Lagos, Nigeria 🏢 Hybrid 👤 Mid–Senior 🕐 Posted 3 days ago
About the role

Flutterwave is looking for a Senior Product Manager to lead our payments team. You'll own the roadmap for our core checkout experience, work closely with engineering and design, and drive decisions using data from millions of transactions processed across Africa every month.

This is a high-ownership role reporting directly to the VP of Product, with real influence over how millions of people and businesses move money across the continent.

What you'll do
  • Own the product roadmap for checkout and payment processing
  • Partner with engineering, design, and data science to ship features that scale across 30+ markets
  • Define and track success metrics for every major release
  • Run customer discovery with merchants ranging from small businesses to enterprise
  • Present roadmap and strategy updates to executive leadership quarterly
What we're looking for
  • 5+ years of product management experience, ideally in fintech or payments
  • Track record of shipping and scaling consumer or B2B products
  • Strong analytical skills — comfortable working directly with SQL and data
  • Excellent written and verbal communication across technical and non-technical audiences
  • Experience working with distributed, cross-functional teams
Benefits
  • Competitive salary, benchmarked to market data on Tutaly
  • Health insurance for you and your dependents
  • Flexible hybrid work arrangement
  • Annual learning and development budget
  • Stock options
